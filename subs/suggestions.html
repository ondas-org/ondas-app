ONDAS grows with the writers who tend it. This is your patch of soil — plant a missing tool, an experimental workflow, or a wild idea no one’s tried to root yet.

Give your seed a name that captures what it does. Short and grounded. You don’t need a full proposal, just something clear enough to take root at a glance.

Walk us through the experience. What does it look like growing in the editor? What does it feel like to use? Even a rough sketch of the interaction helps us tend it.

Context changes everything. Tell us about the moment this would have taken root. The more grounded in your real workflow, the better we can cultivate it.

No seed goes unread. We weigh every submission against what the ecosystem needs most. But not everything can sprout overnight — some concepts need deep roots and real resources to grow properly, and occasionally outside hands to tend. Some features may take time, or bloom in a slightly different form to fit within the platform’s natural constraints.
