Every Command.

INTRODUCTION

Less clicking.
More writing.

Shortcuts are how you write in ONDAS without taking your hands off the keyboard. Type a few characters instead of reaching for a menu, and keep writing. The page never stops moving.

Every shortcut begins the same way: two quotes and four letters, like ""slug. When a command needs extra information, add = and keep going: ""slug=ext. johnny’s house=dusk. Learn the pattern once, and soon you’ll begin predicting shortcuts before you ever need to look them up.
